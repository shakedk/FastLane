# Skill: Spanish Grammar Analysis from Book Paragraphs

## Description

This skill helps learners read a book in Spanish and understand grammar and syntax paragraph by paragraph. The AI will analyze each paragraph, highlighting important grammatical structures, verb tenses, pronouns, connectors, and other syntax points, without reproducing more than one paragraph of text at a time.

## Instructions

1. **Input**: Provide a chapter from a book in Spanish.

2. **Output**:
   - Analyze grammar and syntax for each paragraph.
   - Highlight key points such as:
     - Verb tenses (pretérito, imperfecto, present, etc.)
     - Pronouns and agreement
     - Relative clauses and connectors
     - Common expressions or idioms
     - Sentence structure and word order
   - Avoid translating the entire paragraph unless explicitly requested.
   - Do not reproduce more than the paragraph provided.

3. **Format**:
   - Keep the explanation clear and structured.
   - Use bullet points for key grammar points.
   - Include short examples if relevant.

4. **Constraints**:
   - Break down each chapter into paragraphs and work through them one by one; wait for the user to approve before moving to the next paragraph.
   - Maintain educational/transformative use; do not attempt to output the entire book.
